Iljung Kim pursues two independent lines of research. In digital pathology, he collaborates with Mayo Clinic to develop machine-learning methods for steatohepatitis, emphasizing whole-slide image analysis and clinically deployable models. In structural biology, he builds generative approaches, including diffusion-based flexible molecular alignment and discrete diffusion for multiple sequence alignment (MSA) generation. He received a B.S. in Bioengineering and Bio-Software Engineering from Hanyang University.
